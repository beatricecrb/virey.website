The content engine

One topic. Three pieces.
Every week.

Fully faceless — no face, no voice. Research-first, always. One research session feeds every platform.

01
Research
Pick one topic per week. Go deep — ingredients, studies, brand stories, real data. 30 minutes of genuine research beats 10 posts of surface-level tips.
02
Create
Turn it into 1 carousel + 1 reel + 1 newsletter paragraph. No face, no voice needed. Canva for visuals, ChatGPT for captions.
03
Share
Instagram + Pinterest + TikTok + Substack newsletter. One research session feeds every platform. The newsletter builds the owned audience.
04
Earn
Affiliate links on every post via ManyChat automation. Comment a keyword → receive the link automatically. No selling, no chasing.

What virey stands for

Three things that
never change.

01
Research over trends
Every piece of content starts with genuine research — not what's popular, not what's easy, but what's actually true and useful. If virey can't back it up, virey won't say it.
02
Honest over perfect
Real opinions. Real findings. Affiliate links disclosed. Products only recommended when they'd be recommended to a friend for free. The audience's trust is the only currency that matters.
03
Curious, always
virey exists because genuine curiosity can't be switched off. The topics will evolve, the platform will grow — but the impulse to research something properly and share it honestly never changes.
